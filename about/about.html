About SnackBrief

SnackBrief is your bite-sized creative café — a place where ideas are quick, fun, and fresh. Whether you're an artist, writer, designer, or just someone who loves a good spark of inspiration, this site delivers prompts like tiny digital snacks.

This project was created to explore creativity, user experience, and playful interaction design. More features will be added soon — including custom prompt packs, user submissions, and themed creative challenges.
